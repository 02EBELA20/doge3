Department of Green Energy

DOGE (DEPARTMENT OF GREEN ENERGY) represents an idea rooted in the effort and fight for building eco-friendly and energy-efficient cities of the future. Our goal is to create urban environments where nature and technology coexist in harmony. This vision includes cities powered entirely by renewable energy, buildings covered with green vegetation, fully electrified transportation systems, and infrastructure optimized for sustainability.
